Christmas

Christmas (which means "Feast day of Christ") is a Christian holiday that
refers to the brith of Jesus (whom Christians believe is the Son of God), and
a cultural holiday for non-Christians. The day known as Christmas Day is
celebrated on the 25th day of December.

Santa Claus

Santa Claus, also known as Father Christmas is a legendary figure originating
in Western Christian culture who is said to bring gifts to homes of well-
behaved children on the night of Christmas Eve or during the early morning
hours of Christmas Day.

GOLDEN LAND MYANMAR

Myanmar, formerly known as Burma, is recognized the world as the
Golden Land. It is one of the earliest homes of mankind, where one can
have exclusive experiences of a life-time. Officially, Myanmar is known as
Republic of the Union of Myanmar. Also called Burma, Burmese,
Myanmar, or Pyidaungzu Thamada Myanmar Naingngandaw.

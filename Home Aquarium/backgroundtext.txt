Keeping an aquarium in your home adds numerous benefits to your health, 
like reducing stress and lowering blood pressure. Freshwater tanks tend to be easier 
to care for than saltwater tanks, and its critters are more forgiving to mistakes made 
by new owners. Freshwater fish has its own beautiful selection of colorful and unique 
species that make your aquarium thrive with life.

2. Guppies
